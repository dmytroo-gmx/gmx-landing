Core Philosophy

“No Show, No Pay.”
Trust is built on transactions.

Ratings are locked until the deal is completed and paid. No fake reviews. No paid promotions. Growth first — monetization after critical mass.

01
Free · Growth

Phase 1 — Building a Global Musician Database

100% free for everyone. Musicians create profiles, clients post requests, deals are made. Every transaction builds a verified reputation that cannot be faked or bought.

02
Commission · Scale

Phase 2 — Monetization on Success

A small platform commission is charged only on successfully completed and confirmed deals. Cancelled or disputed — zero. Aligned incentives for all participants.

Secure Transaction Protocol

The Safe Deal System.
Six steps from offer to payout.

Like cash on delivery, but digital and legally binding. Every step is transparent to both parties in real time.

💳

Client Pays

Full amount is transferred to the platform's escrow account. Partial payments are not accepted.

🔒

Funds Frozen

Money is locked. A micro-contract (1 page) is auto-generated and sent to both parties.

🎶

Musician Performs

Event day. The musician shows up and plays. The performance takes place.

🔑

Confirmation Code

Client gives the musician a unique code — or confirms via the app.

💰

Funds Released

Platform transfers payment to the musician. Commission is deducted (Phase 2 only).

⭐

Review Unlocked

Both parties can leave a verified review — tied to this transaction only.

UI/UX Strategy

Complex Logic.
Simple Experience.

Legal and financial complexity is hidden behind clean, progressively disclosed interfaces that guide the user step by step.

01

Progressive Disclosure

Legal and financial steps are broken into small, focused screens. Users see only what's needed now — contract details are available on demand, never forced upfront.

02

Deal Status Front & Center

Every dashboard starts with deal status. A clear visual pipeline (Draft → Paid) instead of confusing notification lists. Color-coded states — instantly clear what needs action.

03

One Primary Action Per Screen

At every critical step — accepting an offer, paying into escrow, entering a code — exactly one prominent button. All secondary options fade to the background.

04

Trust Signals at Every Step

Escrow balance is always visible. Contract is always one tap away. Platform commission is shown before payment confirmation. No surprises, ever.

05

Native Geo Search

Musician search with a map-first interface and radius slider. City autocomplete with a “find musicians near the venue” button. Distance shown in travel hours, not just km.

06

Mobile-First for Musicians

Musicians receive and manage bookings from their phone. Push notifications for offers. One-tap confirmation code entry. Offline gig details view with rider and map.
